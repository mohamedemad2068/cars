The BMW M5 CS
,is the most powerful and exclusive version of the M5 lineup, blending luxury with track-focused performance. Powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 producing 627 horsepower, it rockets from 0–100 km/h in just 3 seconds, making it the fastest M5 ever built. With lightweight carbon fiber components, a tuned chassis, and exclusive details like carbon bucket seats and gold bronze accents, the M5 CS offers a unique balance of daily usability and race-inspired dynamics. It’s a rare, limited-production super sedan that represents the peak of BMW’s M engineering.

The Ferrari SF90 XX
,is a track-focused evolution of the SF90 Stradale, designed to push the boundaries of performance while maintaining its hybrid supercar DNA. Combining a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine with three electric motors, it delivers over 1,000 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful Ferraris ever built. With extreme aerodynamics, lightweight materials, and advanced driving dynamics, the SF90 XX bridges the gap between Ferrari’s road cars and its track-only machines, offering an experience that’s as close to a race car as possible while still being road-legal.

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ Roadster
,is an ultra-high-performance Italian supercar combining blistering power, advanced aerodynamics, and open-top drama. It’s powered by a naturally aspirated V12 engine delivering about 770 CV (566 kW) at 8,500 rpm, capable of sprinting from 0-100 km/h in 2.9 seconds and topping out at over 350 km/h.
